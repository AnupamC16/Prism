Prism OTT Manifest & DRM Demo

Interview demo flow:
  1. Pick a DRM Type (ClearKey, Widevine, FairPlay).
  2. Choose a video file and click Upload & Process. FFmpeg transcodes 4 renditions; Shaka Packager encrypts and writes the manifest.
  3. Click Inspect Manifest to see the real <cenc:pssh> (Widevine/PlayReady) or EXT-X-KEY skd:// (FairPlay) embedded by Shaka.
  4. Click Load Manifest to play. Token issuance, EME challenge, license response, and key delivery are logged in step 4.
  5. ClearKey plays fully in-browser. Widevine/FairPlay need real upstream DRM credentials in .env.
